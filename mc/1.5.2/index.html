Minecraft 1.5.2


Controls:

WASD to move
Space to jump
LSHIFT to crouch
LCTRL to sprint
E to open the inventory
ESC to pause game
Left mouse btn to punch/destroy
Right mouse btn to use an item/entity (eg. doors)
T to open chat
/ to use commands
TAB to view player list (Multiplayer Only)
